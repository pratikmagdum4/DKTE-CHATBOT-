About us

DKTE'S Textile & Engineering Institute, Ichalkaranji (An Autonomous Engineering Colleges in Maharashtra) Affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur

DKTE'S Textile & Engineering Institute, Ichalkaranji (Top Autonomous Engineering College In Maharashtra), established in 1982, as one of the first private Engineering Institutes in Maharashtra, DKTE reflects the vision of leading industrialists and educationalists. The vast exposure and experience of it's founders has helped DKTE to establish it's identity as an Institute of repute in the field of Textile & Engineering College in Maharashtra. The Autonomous Engineering Institute has been awarded “A+” grade by NAAC with 3.53 CGPA in recognition of its overall academic excellence and infrastructure. Also all the eligible UG programmes have been accredited by NBA, New Delhi.

Presently the DKTE (Autonomous Engineering Colleges In Maharashtra) has been catering to Three Diploma Courses, Twelve Undergraduate courses (Five of them in Textiles & Seven in Engineering), Five P.G. Courses & Doctorate programs in Engineering & Textiles and Management studies program. We have around 4000 students and 209 faculty members.

Our institute has emerged as the Winner of 'Best Industry-Linked Engineering Institute-2015 & 2017' twice in Chemical & Allied Engineering Institute Category in the nation-wide survey conducted jointly by AICTE and CII. The institute has also been honored as 'The Best Educational Institute for Textile Engineering' by Ministry of Textiles, GoI, in 2013.

We have Collaborated with many leading Foreign Universities Our Institute has been awarded with many prestigious projects by Govt. of India like Centre of Excellence in Nonwovens, TBI by DST, TIFAC-CORE by DST, TEQIP, etc worth of more than 60 Crore Rupees. Govt. of India, Ministry of Textiles has designated our institute as 'Centre of Excellence in Nonwovens' and has supported with worth of Rs.25.0 Crores to this project.
